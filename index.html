Compass AEPED Consultancy

Compass AEPED is an established urban and regional planning consultancy with over 15 years of expertise in the field of planning and development. Headquartered in Addis Ababa, we are a well-established architecture and engineering consultancy committed to shaping the nation's future through innovative and sustainable design solutions. Founded by a team of esteemed Ethiopian professionals, Compass AEPED is guided by a steadfast commitment to excellence and a profound understanding of the local context.
